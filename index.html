The Spence Guarantee

SATISFACTION GUARANTEED

Your satisfaction is our priority. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your purchase from Spence, simply bring it back in its unaltered original condition with the original receipt within 30 days of the original order date date and we will gladly exchange your purchase or refund your money. Returns on Made To Order rings are subject to a $100 cancellation fee. Sorry, Special Orders are exempt from returns.

The only thing we can't guarantee is that you won't lose your jewelry.
Spence recommends all jewelry be insured.

LIFETIME TRADE-UP ALLOWANCE

Your original Spence center diamond can be fully credited towards a new piece(s) of Spence jewelry at any time, provided the new piece(s) has a combined value of at least 50% greater than your original Spence jewelry purchase price. Simply return your diamond to Spence in its original condition, with the original sales receipt, the Spence Guarantee and the diamond certificate. The Diamond must fall under parameters for Lifetime Protection Against Loss carat weights to qualify for Lifetime Trade-Up Allowance.

Note: Trade-Up credit is only applicable for the value of the original centre diamond, the setting or mounting or your original jewelry will not be credited. Special orders are excluded.

LIFETIME PROTECTION AGAINST LOSS

For Consciously Mined Round, Princess, Oval, Marquise, Pear, Cushion, Emerald and Radiant Diamonds between 0.18 – 3.10 carat weight and Artisan Created Round, Princess, Oval, Marquise , Pear, Cushion, Emerald and Radiant Diamonds between 0.18 – 5.10 carat weight: If your original diamond chips, cracks or separates from its original Spence mounting, and Spence deems that it is in good condition, Spence will replace your diamond once during the lifetime of your ring, free of charge.

To qualify for this benefit:

  • Bring your ring to any Spence location for inspection by a Spence representative every calendar year.
  • All repairs recommended as a result of Spence inspections must be promptly completed by our Spence Studio.
  • Special orders are excluded.

This benefit will become null and void if the ring is repaired or inspected by anyone other than Spence, or if any of the inspection criteria above are not satisfied.

LIFETIME CLEANING AND INSPECTION

Spence will professionally clean and examine your original Spence jewelry, free of charge, anytime you visit one of our locations.

CHANGE YOUR STYLE OF MOUNTING

You may change the style of your original solitaire diamond ring for another style of equal value, free of charge, within one full year of the date of purchase. No refunds will be made on a change of mount that is lower in value than the original mount.

WORKMANSHIP GUARANTEE

Spence rings are handcrafted by our Master Jewelers. We will guarantee your ring Mount against any defects in material and workmanship for one year from the date of purchase. If any defects are found during this time, we will be happy to provide all necessary repairs free of charge.

RING SIZE ADJUSTMENT

We will gladly adjust the size of your original Spence diamond ring free of charge on any one occasion within one year of the date of purchase.
